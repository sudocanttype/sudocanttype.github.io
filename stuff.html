AuditoryUnveil

Do you ever struggle to make out someone's emotions while they're talking with you? Well, this probably can't help with that. However, I present to you Auditory Unveil, a project done in collaboration with the Association of Computing and Machinery. Through the fusion of advanced audio analysis and Convolutional Neural Networks, this project is designed to decode the intricate nuances of human emotion as expressed through speech. By processing audio signals and extracting emotional cues, it can open up a world of applications, from sentiment analysis in auditory customer feedback to developing AI-driven mental health monitoring solutions. Built using Python, Pandas, Tensorflow, Keras, Jupyter Notebook, and a significant amount of 3Blue1Brown.

ScrewQuora

Once upon a time, I googled a question that lead me into Quora(that's how you know you're really in the trenches). I found a related question on the sidebar that seemed to line up perfectly with what I was looking for, and of course I clicked it. Next thing I know, I'm being assaulted by popups left and right, screaming for me to make an account. My blood boiled. Instead of spending 1 minute just making an account, I, in true programmer fashion, spent multiple hours researching and creating this Google Chrome and Firefox-compatible extension that bypasses Quora's "Login to View" accountwall. Screw registration walls.
